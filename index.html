FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting inked.

Get a good night's sleep and eat a full meal before your appointment. Stay hydrated and avoid alcohol for at least 24 hours beforehand. Wear comfortable clothing that allows easy access to the tattoo area. Bring a valid ID and any reference images you'd like to discuss.
Keep the bandage on for 2-4 hours after getting your tattoo. Wash gently with lukewarm water and unscented soap. Apply a thin layer of recommended aftercare ointment 2-3 times daily. Avoid direct sunlight, swimming, and soaking for at least 2 weeks. Don't scratch or pick at the healing skin — let it peel naturally.
Pain levels vary depending on placement and your personal tolerance. Areas with thinner skin or close to bone (ribs, ankles, spine) tend to be more sensitive. Most people describe it as a scratching or vibrating sensation. We'll work with you to ensure your comfort throughout the session.
Pricing depends on the size, complexity, placement, and number of sessions required. Small tattoos start at affordable rates, while larger custom pieces are quoted individually. Contact us on WhatsApp with your design idea and we'll provide a free estimate during consultation.
